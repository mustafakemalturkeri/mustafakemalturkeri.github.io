Featured Projects

Drought Index Calculation and Analysis Software

The software can analyze and visualize drought with the help of many different drought indices (SPI, Palmer Indices, SPEI, etc.). The program is developed in Matlab language. The software includes an extensive statistical tools to analyze statistical characteristics of drought events and a mapping module to create drought hazard and risk maps.

With this much abilities, the program is one of the best drought analyses tools in the world.

Grid Reader - A Unity3D Based Simulation Generator Software with GIS Data

Grid Reader is a simulation designer tool to generate real world simulations in a gaming environment created by Mustafa Kemal Türkeri. Software is written by Python and Unity. Only need some real world digital elevation models, some tree locations and water layers (created in GIS). Then you get a first person experience in the real world!

Konya Basin Drought Management Plan

Positions : Project Engineer
Dates : 2013 – 2015
Client : General Directorate of Water Management – SYGM (Turkey)

Konya Basin Drought Management Plan was the first governmental level drought management plan project in Turkey. Therefore the project included many applications which were the first attempts in Turkey regarding drought management, action plan development for drought, impact assessment, climate change resilience evaluations and drought vulnerability assessment for agriculture, drinking water and industry sectors. Project includes drought management actions until 2050. The project team in Dolsar was responsible for indroducing a methodology for drought management, vulnerability assessments, drought analyses and emergency action plan.
I was a key member of the project team who is responsible for:
• Drought assessments and drought index calculations,
• A comprehensive software development for drought assessment,
• Vulnerability analyses for different key sectors in Konya Basin,
• GIS analyses and evaluations of climate change projections,
• Remote sensing applications,
• Development of an action plan and proposal of a model drought management methodology,
• Web based application design and development.

İstanbul Drinking Water and Wastewater Master Plan

Positions : Climate Change Specialist / GIS Lead
Dates : 2018 – Ongoing
Client : İstanbul Water and Wastewater Administration – İSKİ (Turkey)

İstanbul, which consist around 16 million population, is the largest metropolitan city in Turkey. Because of this condition, Master Plan includes many different aspects. The aim of the project is to define and develop the road map of İSKİ until 2053. I was a key person in the project because of my abilities regarding climate change resilience and GIS. I have completed a climate change and drought impact and vulnerability assessment report.
In the project, I was responsible for:
• Comprehensive trend analyses,
• Climate change impact assessment and applications for increase the climate change resilience and adaptive capacity,
• Development of an action plan and emergency response plan for İstanbul city,
• Statistical downscaling and analyses of around 40 different future climate projections,
• Vulnerability assessments for drinking water, agriculture, industry and health sectors.
• Development different climate change impact scenarios based on projections. 

Kızılırmak Basin Master Plan Project

Positions : Project Engineer / GIS Lead
Dates : 2015 – 2018
Client : General Directorate of State Hydraulic Works – DSİ (Turkey) 

With around 82,000 km² area, Kızılırmak Basin is the second largest basin in Turkey. With the aspects of total count of facilities however, Kızılırmak Basin is the first basin in Turkey. The project consists of evaluation of basin in terms of irrigation, drinking water and energy production, and assessment of the current conditions, then provide recommendations for the future basin development actions until 2050. I worked as a project engineer in the project as well as I was responsible for the GIS works in the project.
I was responsible for:
• GIS applications and analyses for Kızılırmak Basin,
• Inventory development and data analyses,
• Population estimation and software development,
• Naturalization of river flows,
• Operation studies for different facilities
